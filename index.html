How to Play

  1. Choose a level on the home screen.
  2. Add your players.
  3. Spin the wheel to pick the judge.
  4. The judge gets up to 3 questions (2 re-rolls), and must hold to reveal it.
  5. Use guess mode or reveal mode if you want extra chaos.
  6. End game to see the final history + custom questions.
